Hum Kaun Hain

Welcome to The RAILSPK ("hum", "humara", "site"). Humari website address hai: https://therails.pk. Hum Pakistan Railways ke vlogs, reviews, aur updates ke liye dedicated hain.

Hum Kya Data Collect Karte Hain

Jab aap humari site visit karte hain, hum kuch maloomat collect kar sakte hain:

  • Email Address: Agar aap humare "Reach Out" form (jo `index.html` par hai) ke zariye humse raabta karte hain, toh hum aap ka email address save karte hain taake aap ko jawab de sakein.

Cookies

Humari site behtareen user experience ke liye cookies ka istemal kar sakti hai. Cookies choti text files hoti hain jo aap ke computer par save hoti hain. Yeh humein analytics data track karne mein madad deti hain.

Third-Party Links (YouTube waghera)

Humari website par YouTube ke videos embedded hain aur humare social media pages (Facebook, Instagram, etc.) ke links hain. Jab aap in links par click karte hain, toh aap unn websites par chale jate hain. Unki apni privacy policies hoti hain, jinke hum zimmedar nahi hain.

Aap Ke Data Ki Hifazat

Hum aap ke data ki hifazat ke liye munasib iqdamaat (reasonable steps) uthate hain. Lekin, internet par koi bhi method 100% mehfooz nahi hota.
